The Hidden Dangers of Delayed Water Removal

When water infiltrates your Denver home, the clock starts ticking. Within the first 24 hours:

  • Drywall begins to swell and break down
  • Metal surfaces start to tarnish
  • Furniture wood begins to swell
  • Mold can begin forming in as little as 24-48 hours
  • Structural wood components start absorbing water

The Denver Climate Factor

Health Risks of Standing Water

Standing water isn't just a structural concern. It creates serious health risks:

  • Bacterial growth
  • Mold spores
  • Allergen development
  • Indoor air quality issues
