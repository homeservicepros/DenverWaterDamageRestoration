Professional Water Removal Methods for Denver Properties

At Denver Water Damage Restoration Company, we employ these proven techniques to protect homes across Capitol Hill and Five Points:

1. Advanced Water Extraction

Our industrial-grade submersible pumps and truck-mounted extraction units remove standing water quickly. This is crucial for our water damage cleanup services in Denver, especially during our intense summer thunderstorms.

2. Specialized Drying Equipment

  • High-velocity air movers
  • Commercial dehumidifiers
  • Thermal imaging cameras
  • Moisture meters

3. Targeted Moisture Detection

We use advanced moisture mapping technology to ensure no hidden water remains in your walls or under flooring. This is particularly important for our flooded basement cleanup services.

4. Controlled Demolition When Necessary

Sometimes, removing affected materials is the most effective solution. Our team carefully removes damaged drywall and flooring to prevent mold growth.

5. Specialized Floor Drying Systems

For hardwood floors common in Cheesman Park homes, we use specialized floor mat systems that extract moisture from below.

6. Structural Cavity Drying

Wall cavities and crawl spaces require special attention. Our team uses wall injection drying systems to reach hidden moisture.

7. Dehumidification Protocols

Denver's varying humidity levels require precise dehumidification control. We adjust our equipment based on daily conditions to achieve optimal drying times.
